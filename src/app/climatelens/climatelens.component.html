Introduction

For this project, we were tasked with picking an upcoming technology and to create a product with it that addressed a current social issue. Our team chose the technology of augmented reality to address the social issue of climate change.

Mission Statement

Our solution will use AR simulations of climate change disasters and steps on how to avoid that future specifically tailored to the userâ€™s location, to spur individuals to actively participate in the fight against climate change.

Logo

To reflect our app's goal of providing a closer look at the world around us through AR, the logo was created with the idea of looking at our world through a lens.

Lean Canvas

The lean canvas acted as our scaffolding of the project, fleshing out the idea and goals of the product.

The Problem

The problem is not lack of awareness anymore, but lack of urgency about climate change.

Value Proposition

We defined the value our product would bring to our target audience. This helped shape the features we would like out product to provide in relation to what our users would want.

Our Solution

To give users the tools, resources, and motivation to reduce their individual negative impact on the environment with a central information hub and community for activists.

User Journey

The journey we envisioned for users was one where they started curious and ended with motivation to take action. Our app would provide the resources to make acting on their motivation as easy and accessible as possible. The type of resources would range from personal tips for being more environment conscious to volunteer opportunities in their community.
